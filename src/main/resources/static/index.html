Help the Critters to find the Mutants

The Critters are a peaceable, yet unknown colony living in the forest next to the Smurfs. They have build small houses where they live in peace and harmony.

But recently a black wizard found the Critters and sent some mutants to the Critters, that look quite identically as the Critters. Since the critters cannot distinguish between a mutated and non mutated Critter, they need your help!
On the way to a save tower, they behave according to some rules, but the mutated ones do not now how to behave correctly. It's your task, to place some magic spots on their way, that check the correct behaviour of the Critters and charm the mutated ones away.

Help the to save the peace of the Critters. Start with the first level or select another level.
